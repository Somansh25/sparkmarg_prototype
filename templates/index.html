Frequently Asked Questions

What is the SparkMarg Simulation Engine?

SparkMarg is an immersive platform designed to simulate real-world technical and leadership scenarios. It allows professionals to build skills in a zero-risk environment through branching narrative tracks.

How are my competency scores calculated?

Each decision you make within a simulation has weighted impacts on four core vectors: Leadership, Technical Execution, Problem Solving, and Strategic Communication. These are aggregated to build your professional profile.

Can I resume a simulation later?

Yes! SparkMarg automatically saves your progress at every node. You can find your active runs in the Dashboard and resume them at any time.
